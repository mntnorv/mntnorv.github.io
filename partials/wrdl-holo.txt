[[bu;;]wrdl-holo] (https://github.com/mntnorv/wrdl-holo)

A Boggle-like Android game in Holo

[[u;;]Features:]

 - Holo-style interface
 - Grids of multiple sizes (4x4, 5x5, ...)
 - Multiple infinite games (no need to end one to start another)

[[u;;]Game modes:]

 - Infinite (find as many words as you can)
 - Timed (score as many points as you can in a fixed time period):
   - 1 minute
   - 3 minutes
   - 5 minutes
